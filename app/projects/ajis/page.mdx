## Overview

I designed and developed an academic journal website for the American Journal of Irish Studies (AJIS), creating a more engaging way to browse Irish and Irish-American publications through scroll-based interactions, article layouts, and clearer editorial structure. Launching Fall 2026!

Built with Javascript, Flask, Jinja templates, and article content generated from Markdown. Scrollytelling articles use Scrollama with maps rendered using MapLibre GL JS.
